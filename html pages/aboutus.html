About Us

Medi Safe Hospital

Welcome to Medi Safe Hospital, where we combine advanced medical care with a compassionate approach to health and healing. Located in Colombo, Kandy and Galle. we are dedicated to providing comprehensive healthcare services across various specialties, including cardiology, cancer, and eye surgon. Our team of experienced professionals is committed to delivering personalized, patient-centered care in a state-of-the-art facility. At Medi Safe Hospital, your health and well-being are our top priority.
